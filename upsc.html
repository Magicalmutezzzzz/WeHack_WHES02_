UPSC Civil Services Exam - Complete Guide

What is UPSC?

The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) conducts the Civil Services Examination (CSE) to recruit candidates for prestigious positions like IAS, IPS, IFS, and other Group A & B services in India.

Eligibility Criteria

Age Limit: 21-32 years (General), with relaxation for reserved categories.
Educational Qualification: Bachelor's degree from a recognized university.
Number of Attempts: 6 (General), 9 (OBC), Unlimited (SC/ST).

UPSC Exam Stages

1. **Preliminary Exam (Prelims)** - Objective type, consists of General Studies Paper I & CSAT.
2. **Main Exam (Mains)** - Descriptive type, consists of 9 papers.
3. **Personality Test (Interview)** - Face-to-face interaction with the UPSC panel.

UPSC Exam Syllabus

**Prelims:**
- General Studies Paper 1: History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Science, Current Affairs.
- CSAT (Paper 2): Logical reasoning, Mathematics, Reading comprehension.

**Mains:**
- Essay Paper.
- 4 General Studies Papers (History, Polity, Economy, Ethics, etc.).
- 2 Optional Subject Papers.
- 2 Language Papers.

UPSC Exam Pattern

**Prelims:** 2 Papers (200 marks each).
**Mains:** 9 Papers (Total 1750 marks).
**Interview:** 275 marks.
**Final Selection:** Based on Mains + Interview (2025 marks).
