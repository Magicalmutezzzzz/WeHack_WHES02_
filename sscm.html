SSC MTS Exam Information

About SSC MTS

The Staff Selection Commission - Multi Tasking Staff (SSC MTS) Exam is conducted to recruit candidates for various non-technical Group C posts in different government departments.

Eligibility Criteria

Education: Matriculation (10th Pass) from a recognized board.

Age Limit: 18-25 years (relaxations as per government norms).

Exam Pattern

  • Paper 1: Computer-Based Test (General Intelligence, Reasoning, Numerical Aptitude, General English, General Awareness)
  • Paper 2: Descriptive Test (Short Essay/Letter Writing)
  • Physical Test: Only for Havaldar posts

How to Apply

Visit the official SSC website to register and apply for the SSC MTS examination.
