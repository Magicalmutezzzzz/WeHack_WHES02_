SSC CGL Exam Information

About SSC CGL

The Staff Selection Commission - Combined Graduate Level (SSC CGL) Exam is conducted to recruit staff for various posts in ministries, departments, and organizations of the Government of India.

Eligibility Criteria

Education: Bachelor's degree from a recognized university.

Age Limit: Varies by post (usually between 18-32 years).

Exam Pattern

  • Tier 1: Computer-Based Test (General Awareness, Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude, English)
  • Tier 2: Subject-Specific Papers
  • Tier 3: Descriptive Test
  • Tier 4: Skill Test/Computer Proficiency Test (if applicable)

How to Apply

Visit the official SSC website to register and apply for the SSC CGL examination.
